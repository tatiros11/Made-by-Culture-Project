About

Our Mission

Welcome to Made by Culture, a dynamic online magazine that celebrates the intersection of STEM and the arts. We are a platform created by and for students and young adults in the greater Los Angeles and Glendale areas, showcasing the incredible achievements, opportunities, and events that shape our communities. At Made by Culture, we believe in the power of both scientific and artistic innovation. Our platform highlights journalistic articles and offers a space for youth to submit their own creative works—from poetry and research papers to photography, art, and design—giving them an opportunity to share their talents with the world. Our mission is to bridge the gap between innovation and creativity by embracing the importance of both STEM and the humanities. By doing so, we hope to inspire the next generation of thinkers and creators, helping them to understand that both fields are essential to shaping our future.
